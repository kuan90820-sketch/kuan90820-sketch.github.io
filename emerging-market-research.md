## Project Overview

During my internship at ITRI IEK's Emerging Markets Group, I worked on industry research related to emerging economies. One research task focused on estimating the Vietnam electric commercial vehicle market under limited-data conditions.

## Research Approach

The project combined macroeconomic data, industry reports, vehicle-market information, policy context, and reasonable assumptions to construct a market-sizing framework.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Market sizing under limited information
- Emerging economy research
- Policy and industry context analysis
- Structured report writing
